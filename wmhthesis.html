Deep Learning Methods for the Segmentation of White Matter Hyperintesities

Master's thesis project on Medical Imaging Analysis

I was a guest at "Pioneer Center for AI" in Copenhagen. The project was aimed at developing a robust method for the segmentation of age-related white matter hyperintesities, a biomarker for cognitive decline and increased risk of mortality.

A part of the results has been accepted and published at the MIDL 2023 conference, in Nashville, in the short paper track.
